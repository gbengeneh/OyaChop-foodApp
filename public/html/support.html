Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order on your website?

To place an order, simply browse our menu, select the items you want, and add them to your cart. Then proceed to the checkout page to complete your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, online wallets, and cash on delivery (in eligible locations).

Do you offer free delivery?

We may offer free delivery for orders that meet a certain minimum amount. Otherwise, a nominal delivery fee may apply based on your location.

What if I receive the wrong item or my order is incorrect?

We apologize for any inconvenience caused. In such cases, please get in touch with our support team immediately, and we will rectify the issue promptly.

What if I need to modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order, but the availability of these options depends on the status of your order and the time left before delivery. Please contact our customer support as soon as possible for assistance.
